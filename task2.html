Samantha Ruth Prabhu is a prominent Indian actress known for her work in Tamil and Telugu films. She made her acting debut in 2010 with the Telugu film "Ye Maaya Chesave," which garnered critical acclaim. Over the years, she has received several awards, including four Filmfare Awards. Samantha is celebrated for her versatile roles in films like "Rangasthalam" and "Oh! Baby." Besides acting, she is also an entrepreneur and philanthropist, actively involved in various charitable activities through her organization, Pratyusha Support.

Dhanush, born as Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja, is a prominent Indian actor known for his work in Tamil cinema. He gained widespread recognition with his film "Aadukalam" (2011), for which he won the National Film Award for Best Actor. Apart from acting, Dhanush is a talented playback singer and lyricist, famous for the viral song "Why This Kolaveri Di." He has also made his mark in Bollywood with movies like "Raanjhanaa" (2013). Additionally, Dhanush is the son-in-law of the legendary Indian actor Rajinikanth.
